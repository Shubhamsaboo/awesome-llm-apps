Senior AI Engineer

We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer to help build practical AI products for internal operations and customer-facing workflows. You will design and ship Python services, integrate LLM APIs, and work closely with product, design, and operations teams.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain Python and FastAPI services for AI-enabled workflows.
- Design prompts, evaluations, and monitoring for LLM features.
- Collaborate with product managers and stakeholders to turn business requirements into reliable AI tools.
- Improve data processing pipelines using SQL, PostgreSQL, and cloud services.
- Mentor engineers on AI development practices and production readiness.
- Document technical decisions and communicate trade-offs clearly.

Requirements:
- 5+ years of software engineering experience.
- Strong Python, FastAPI, SQL, and API design skills.
- Experience with OpenAI or similar LLM APIs.
- Familiarity with AWS, Docker, CI/CD, and production observability.
- Good stakeholder communication and ownership.
- Bonus: experience with RAG, vector databases, LangChain, or evaluation frameworks.
