Claude Limits

Reads your usage from claude.ai using your existing browser session. Nothing leaves this browser except the request to claude.ai itself.

15 min

Legal notice

This extension calls internal, undocumented endpoints on claude.ai using your logged-in browser cookies. Anthropic has not published these endpoints and may change them without notice. This is a personal-use tool for reading your own limits; use at your own risk.

Endpoints used

  • GET https://claude.ai/api/organizations
  • GET https://claude.ai/api/organizations/{org_id}/usage
  • GET https://claude.ai/api/organizations/{org_id}/overage_spend_limit
  • GET https://claude.ai/api/account
